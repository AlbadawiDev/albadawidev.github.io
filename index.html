Sample case - WordPress speed improvement

Before on mobile
  • LCP 5.3 seconds
  • INP 280 ms and CLS 0.21
  • Page weight 3.2 MB
  • Requests 90
  • Lighthouse score 42 out of 100
After on mobile
  • LCP 1.9 seconds
  • INP 120 ms and CLS 0.03
  • Page weight 1.1 MB and 48 requests
  • Lighthouse score 89 out of 100
What I change in this type of job
  • Convert images to lighter formats and proper sizes with lazy loading.
  • Set up cache and minification with tools like LiteSpeed or Cloudflare.
  • Use defer or async on scripts and inline critical CSS.
  • Add preconnect and preload for key fonts and CDNs.
  • Fix contact forms and email sending.
  • Clean up the database and remove unused options.
